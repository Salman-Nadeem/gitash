Sailing Boats


Traditionally, many different kinds of boats have been used as fishing boats to catch fish in the sea,
 or on a lake or river. Even today, many traditional fishing boats are still in use. According to the United Nations
Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO), at the end of 2004, the world fishing fleet consisted of about 4 million
vessels, of which 2.7 million were undecked (open) boats.While nearly all decked vessels were mechanised,
only one-third of the undecked fishing boats were powered, usually with outboard engines.
 The remaining 1.8 million boats were traditional craft of various types, operated by sail and oars.
